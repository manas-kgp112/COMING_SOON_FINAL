Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive

The Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive, one of the massive flagship events undertaken by the Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur is one of its kind initiative held in 25+ cities consistently over the years across the nation in order to stimulate the spirit of entrepreneurship and encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their own venture. It will be held around the first fortnight of October at a reputable college in the host city. It consists of a series of guest lectures by notable entrepreneurs, followed by workshops and a competition engaging the students in multifaceted domains of knowledge.
PS: This year we are back with the offline edition of Entrepreneurship Awareness Drive !


Various events that you can be part of during the event are:


  • Keynote Sessions

  • Workshops

  • Fireside Chat

Local Startups' Meet

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur has successfully influenced students who are enthusiastic about the startup ecosystem since 2006. With our aim to influence people who have already ventured into creating their own enterprise we proudly launch the 5th chapter of the flagship initiative Local Startups Meet (LSM) 2022. LSM 2022 will be held around the first fortnight of October in 11 Cities. Local Startups Meet is an Invite-only, well-curated event, chiefly embodying eminent Investors, venture capitalists, and Influencers along with the early-stage startups, and some incubated startups that are currently working on their Alpha or Beta stage of product development. The event is designed to provide startups in Tier 1 cities with Networking and pitching opportunities.
PS: This year we are back with the offline edition of Local Startups Meet !

The event comprises the following sessions:


  • Panel Discussion

  • Workshops

  • Networking Session

  • Pitching Session

  • Mentoring Session (To be held in Online mode)
